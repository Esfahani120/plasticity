Function that creates the contribution of the specified quadrature point to the local K, M, and G matrices and F and H vectors. K and F are associated with the standard (nodal) dofs, M and H are associated with the enhanced (interior) dofs, and G containes the cross terms. The deformation gradient, Kirchhoff stress, and elastoplastic tangent are needed, as well as the quadrature point number.
